Tutorials

Minuet in G
Minuet in g is a timeless classical piece that every aspiring classical musisian must know. It follows a simple and sweet tune that is simple and easy to comprehend and enjoyable to listen to.

Vivaldi Spring
Composed by Antonio Vivaldi, this is piece is still very commonly heard today. It still retains its importance through its flawless tone and impeccably elegant arrangement.

Andantino
Andantino is a piece in G major composed by Curelli. His songs are well known for their simple yet creative style. I am sure you will enjoy learning this piece.

Ode to Joy
No doubt you are well aquainted with this song. This song, also known as Beethoven's 9th symphony, is simple and easy to master. Bringing about harder variants is also equally enjoyable.

Fur Elise
Fur Elise is another very very famous composition of Beethoven's. It was actually the set ringtone just a few decades back! It is a must know song for most musicians. Easy and quick to master.

Spanish Romance
Spanish Romance also known as Romanza is one of the most famous pieces composed by virtuoso composer Fransisco Tarrega. It's repetitive tone is elegant and soothing. However, this piece takes a white to master.

Jesu, Joy of Man's Desiring
One of the more famous pieces of Baroque composer Bach, this piece was usually played in churches. It utilizes a complex combination of chords and melody which is awesome to know.

Etude in B minor
Although not very famous it works as the perfect practice song for an intermediate guitarist. Its creative tone makes it slightly hard to learn.

Carcassi Opus 60 no 7
In the classical community, this song is one of the more famous pieces composed by Carcassi. It uses a simplified form of tremolo which is a great prelude to learning the real technique.

Prelude
Prelude, also known by the actual name Bach's cello suite no. 1 is one of the most famous and almost overplayed classical pieces of all time. However, it is not very hard and is fun to learn.

Asturias
Asturias is a very hard piece. It will definitely be a challenge for most guitarists.

Recuerdos de la Alhambra
The most famous piece composed by Fransisco Tarrega. Uses a technique called tremolo which he invented. If you can learn this, you are the pro!

Bourree in E minor
Bourree in E minor is one of Bach's famous and often played pieces. A familiar tune to the accustomed ear, it is hard but not impossible.

The Frog Galliard
A very famous classical composed in the 20th Century by composer John Dowland.

Libertango
Libertango is yet another famous classical piece composed by Astor Piazzolla.
